  • Elevator Operation Data Record
Background

Elevator operation data, such as the real-time height of the elevator, up & down signal, alarm status, and emergency power status, they are all useful for running status monitoring, fault prediction, fault handling and accident rescue.

Solutions

By installing sensors in elevators and elevator hoistway, the key data needed for maintenance can be collected and uploaded to the CPChain platform.

By utilizing the characteristics of Blockchain, in which data can be traced back but cannot be tampered with, the data record of elevator movement and the snapshot of key maintenance operation are stored on the chain, to provide reliable records for ACI, insurance investigation and quality assessment of maintenance.
